A graphical representation of ideas and concepts that actually represent how the mind works is a mind map.

It is the best way to think of new ideas, or build on existing concepts, plan and present ideas and also, solve problems. Without worrying about any order or structure, a mind map allows you to represent your thoughts and ideas that are linked with a central, primary concept or prompt.
